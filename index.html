About REC

Rajalakshmi Engineering College, an autonomous institution affiliated with Anna University, was established in 1997 and has grown into one of Tamil Nadu's leading engineering colleges. Committed to excellence in education, REC provides state-of-the-art facilities and focuses on holistic student development through innovative teaching methods and industry collaboration.

About ECE Department

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at REC has been consistently delivering excellence in education since its inception. With cutting-edge laboratories, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections, we prepare students for the evolving world of technology. Our department focuses on areas like VLSI, Embedded Systems, Communication Systems, and Signal Processing.
